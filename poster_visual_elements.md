## POSTER COLOR SCHEME RECOMMENDATIONS

### Primary Colors:
- **Title/Header:** Dark Blue (#003366) or Navy (#1a237e)
- **Section Headers:** Medium Blue (#1976d2)
- **Accent/Highlights:** Orange/Red for alerts (#f44336)
- **Background:** Light Gray (#f5f5f5) or White
- **Text:** Dark Gray (#424242) or Black

### Color Coding by Section:
- **Abstract:** Light Blue tint
- **Introduction:** Light Green tint
- **Objectives/Scope:** Light Yellow tint
- **Methodology:** Light Purple tint
- **Results:** Light Green tint
- **Challenges/Ethics:** Light Orange tint (attention)
- **References:** Neutral Gray

### Data Visualization Colors:
- **Normal activity:** Green tones
- **Anomalies:** Red/Orange tones
- **Alerts:** Red (#f44336)
- **Baselines:** Blue tones

## TYPOGRAPHY RECOMMENDATIONS

### Font Hierarchy:
1. **Title:** Sans-serif, Bold, 48-60pt (e.g., Arial Bold, Helvetica Bold)
2. **Section Headers:** Sans-serif, Bold, 24-28pt
3. **Subsection Headers:** Sans-serif, Semi-bold, 18-20pt
4. **Body Text:** Sans-serif, Regular, 12-14pt (e.g., Arial, Helvetica)
5. **Captions:** Sans-serif, Italic, 10-11pt

### Font Suggestions:
- **Primary:** Arial or Helvetica (widely available, clear)
- **Alternative:** Calibri, Verdana (good readability)
- **Avoid:** Serif fonts (Times New Roman) for body text (less readable at distance)
